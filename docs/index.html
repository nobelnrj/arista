about us

Arista is basically a dream come true. I have always envisioned a society that is self-aware and that is not hesitant to ask for help. When I met my partner with same motivation, an urge for positive change and a heart to help others, Arista was born. This is our first step towards a lifelong goal.

The word arista means happiness and security. Isn't it all we look for in our everyday lives? True to its name we want to provide a safe space for every individual to vent their thoughts and emotions irrespective of their differences. This venting is done to trained professionals in a judgement free zone who will help them to cope better in their everyday lives

Problems big or small are given the equal importance at arista. We help the identity their unhelpful ways of thinking or feeling and help them modify it. This is done through online telephonic and offline counselling sessions and training program.

Mental health is not taken seriously until it is extreme. But small stresses at everyday lives have significant impact in the overall wellbeing. We at Arista focus on removing these stressors and other blocks to bring a positive on their lives

Psycho-education is one of our primary goals. We strive to help people understand themselves better and provide adequate mental health awareness. This in order promotes self-love and paves a way to a fulfilled life with more efficient coping skills, We ultimately look forward to spread a little love and harvest healthy minds.

I'm a Consultant Psychologist with a Masters in Psychology from Presidency College, Chennai. I have a Diploma in Counselling and LifeCoaching and have obtained my CBT and REBT Certification with CTAA accreditation. I'm always curious to learn more and have done various certificate courses such including Child Therapy, Hypnotherapy, Current trends in Psychology etc. Through Arista, I'm fulfilling my goal to psychoeducate everyone and provide mental health care at an affordable rate.
